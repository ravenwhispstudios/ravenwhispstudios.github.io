Project

Bound By Death

In the elven kingdom of Lýndoril, a desperate king defied the natural order to save his newborn daughter, Lýriel. A forbidden ritual chained her soul to Death itself, turning the reaper into her eternal shadow. Years later, both kingdom and child pay the price of that choice.

Bound By Death is a cooperative isometric hack & slash focused on tight combat and narrative. One player controls Lýriel, agile and precise, while the other becomes Death, a spectral force that bends the battlefield with control, executions and pure inevitability.

Together they explore ritual chambers, haunted forests and war-torn cities, hunted by those who want to destroy what never should have existed. Lýriel seeks a way to reclaim her life; Death seeks balance in a world that tried to cheat it.

Co-op hack & slash Isometric camera Dark fantasy narrative PC prototype
Gameplay pillars
  • Duo combat: intertwined abilities between Lýriel & Death
  • Risk & reward: powerful rituals with lasting costs
  • Co-op first: designed to be experienced together
  • Atmosphere: strong art direction and soundscape
Target experience
A short, replayable adventure where each run reveals more about the pact between Lýriel and Death, and the true consequences of binding a mortal soul to the inevitable.
